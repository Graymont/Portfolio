Overview

The Factory plugin is a Spigot plugin that introduces advanced automation to Minecraft. Players can build different types of machines like Furnaces, Crushers, and Auto-Crafters to process materials efficiently. and also with custom steampunk style RPG Systems (with items and abilities)
